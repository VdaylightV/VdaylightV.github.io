The Log of Second Week

Posted on 2020-09-18 Edited on 2020-09-19 In Diary
Symbols count in article: 1.8k Reading time ≈ 2 mins.

Dear Diary:

Now this is the second week of this new semester. The time has been passed so fast that I feel I cannot grasp it. It is some kind of weird. Now I can just list some things that happened to me:

  • Lost my favorite umbrella
  • Website has been cracked

These things above annoyed me most, especially the second one, which took me a whole afternoon to fix and rebuild it.

Given the condition that the new umbrella which is the same as the old one has been sent to me, I feel a little better.

  • Every night chat to my angel
  • Arrange with my angel to travel together in National Day Holiday.

I feel so fortunate that I met such an adorable and nice girl. It is such a wonderful thing to chat with her. She thinks highly of a righteous, virtuous and valorous person. And I determine to try my best to be such a person. Hahaha, it seems that I have been more open and clear since I met her. Moreover, I gradually see the light and target in the misty world. For the first time since I entered college, I have such a strong feeling——I want to try my best to improve myself and become a better man. Just as the quotation from the famous movie The Notebook says: The best love is the kind that awakens the soul and makes us reach for more, that plants a fire in our hearts and brings peace to our minds. Maybe that is what I have felt.

  • Hesitated to arrange my courses

Now I have finally finally the arrangement of my courses. Just as what I have planned before this semester, I will practice my coding ability to ensure that I will be qualified t o get a job future. Now I will focus all my attention on my projects and coding home work to make myself a better coder.

  • Learning Machine Learning and Computer Vision

I did spend some time on these things this week. However, apparently it is not enough, I have to pay more attention on it.

  • Reading Little Women

I think this is such a good book which describes the story of four sisters and their growth. After I watched the $movie$ version, I became quite interested in it. The affection in the story is what I long for.

This is my second week of this semester, it seems that things have become better. And I will to try my best to a righteous, virtuous and valorous man.

See you next week.

​ Nector Gan

​ Friday, 19th September
